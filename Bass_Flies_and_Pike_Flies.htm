Rabbit Strip Dahlberg Diver

The Rabbit Strip Dahlberg Diver is tied with a weed guard. This fly is used to catch large predatory fish such as Northern Pike and large Bass. This fly is an effective pattern fished in shallows or in water with weeds just under the surface. Top water hits are spectacular. Given the proper stripping action this fly performs in an erratic manner that entices feeding fish to strike. ( hook size #4 )

Lefty's Deceiver

Lefty's Deceiver is a fly for large predator fish. This classic saltwater pattern revolutionized saltwater fly fishing. Lefty Kreh designed this pattern for fly fishing stripers in the Chesapeake Bay. Clouser Minnow

The Clouser Minnow is a classic fly that can be used for catching most predatory fish. The retrieve should be jerky. The metal eyes make it a relatively heavy fly that constantly drops and rises as it is stripped through the water. This action is just like a frightened baitfish trying to escape. Very attractive to pike and musky. ( hook size # 1/0 )
